Friday, April 3

Dijon & Lyon

Drive ~2 hours (Dijon to Lyon)
Overnight Hôtel Maison Lacassagne
Distance ~200 km

Morning Stop: Dijon

Location: 15 km from Château de Longecourt

Timing: 2-3 hours (9:00-11:30 AM)

Parking: Darcy parking (underground, central location)

Top 5 Things to See:

  • Ducal Palace (Palais des Ducs) - Place de la Libération, stunning semicircular square
    Climb Tour Philippe le Bon (316 steps) for panoramic views | FREE entry
    Apr-Oct 9:00-12:00, 13:45-17:30
  • Owl Trail (Parcours de la Chouette) - Follow brass owl markers through old town
    Rub the lucky owl sculpture on Notre-Dame church with left hand!
    Free map at tourist office or follow markers
  • Notre-Dame de Dijon - 13th century Gothic church with famous gargoyles
  • Rue des Forges - Medieval street with half-timbered houses
    Hôtel Chambellan at #34 (15th century mansion)
  • Les Halles Market - Designed by Gustave Eiffel (Tue-Sat mornings only)
    Try Dijon mustard from Moutarde Maille boutique nearby

Travel Notes

Compact old town - everything walkable in 30 minutes. Kids will enjoy following the owl trail. Grab coffee and croissant at Place de la Libération before exploring. Leave by 11:30-12:00 to reach Lyon by early afternoon.

Exploring Lyon - Activities & Highlights

  • Vieux Lyon (Old Town) - UNESCO World Heritage site with Renaissance architecture
  • Traboules - Explore Lyon's famous hidden passageways
  • Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière - Hilltop basilica with panoramic city views
  • Parc de la Tête d'Or - Beautiful urban park with lake, zoo, and botanical gardens
  • Lyon Museum of Fine Arts
  • Evening stroll along the riverbanks - Lyon has beautiful illuminations

Dining

Lyon is considered the gastronomic capital of France. Consider a bouchon lyonnais (traditional restaurant).

Practical Tips

Lyon parking can be challenging - research parking garages near accommodation. Consider Lyon City Card for transport and museum access. Fourvière funicular makes hilltop access easy.

Accommodation: Park Hotel Villa Salzea

Address: Via Duca Degli Abruzzi 29, Trofarello, Italy

Website: villasalzea.it

Location: Trofarello, less than 10 km from Turin center

A charming 3-star hotel housed in a restored 18th-century aristocratic villa, formerly the residence of Count Negri. Set in a large, exquisite park featuring a centuries-old beech tree.

Amenities:

  • Restaurant on-site — dinner available
  • Swimming pool (seasonal/summer)
  • Free private parking
  • Free WiFi, air-conditioning, garden views
  • Historic park — perfect for an evening stroll

Activities

  • Settle in and explore the villa grounds and historic park
  • Dinner at the hotel restaurant
  • Optional evening drive into Turin (~15 min) for a stroll through Piazza Castello

Route Considerations

Mont Blanc tunnel or Fréjus tunnel route from Lyon. Check tunnel traffic and tolls in advance. Trofarello is ideally positioned for the next day's drive to Bologna (~300 km, ~3 hours).

Trofarello to Bologna

Drive ~3-4 hours
Overnight Corte del Sant'Orsola (3 nights)
Distance ~320 km

Exploring Bologna

  • Piazza Maggiore - Historic heart with Basilica di San Petronio
  • Due Torri (Two Towers) - Climb Torre degli Asinelli (498 steps) for views
  • Archiginnasio - Historic university building with anatomical theater
  • Porticoes of Bologna - UNESCO site, 38km of covered walkways
  • Santo Stefano complex - Medieval 'Seven Churches' complex
  • Quadrilatero market area - Vibrant food market district
  • Via dell'Indipendenza - Main shopping street

Dining in Bologna

  • Trattoria Anna Maria - Authentic traditional Bolognese cuisine
  • Osteria dell'Orsa - Popular with locals, excellent tagliatelle al ragù
  • All'Osteria Bottega - Wine bar with local dishes
  • Mercato di Mezzo - Food hall for casual dining
  • Gelato: Cremeria Funivia or Stefino

Must Try:

  • Tagliatelle al ragù (authentic Bolognese sauce)
  • Tortellini in brodo
  • Mortadella
  • Cotoletta alla bolognese

Accommodation: Corte del Sant'Orsola

Address: Via Giuseppe Massarenti, 4, 40138 Bologna, Italy

Website: lacortedelsantorsola.com

Phone: +39 375 648 3969

Day Trip to Venice

Travel Train from Bologna Centrale to Venezia Santa Lucia (~1.5-2 hours)
Overnight Bologna

Activities in Venice

  • St. Mark's Square and Basilica - The iconic heart of Venice
  • Doge's Palace - Gothic masterpiece and seat of Venetian power
  • Rialto Bridge - Historic bridge over the Grand Canal
  • Grand Canal vaporetto ride - See Venice from the water
  • Explore quieter neighborhoods - Cannaregio, Dorsoduro

Important

Book Venice train tickets in advance for best prices. Trains run frequently from Bologna Centrale. Allow a full day for exploring Venice.

Switzerland to Brussels

Long Driving Day

Long driving day - recommend early start. Share driving if possible. Plan fuel stops and rest breaks in advance.

Potential Stop Options

  • Luxembourg City - Compact, easy to explore on a stopover
  • Reims - Champagne region, cathedral, quick visit possible
  • Strasbourg - If taking the eastern route

Essential Planning Notes

Documents:

  • Valid passport/ID for all travelers
  • Vehicle registration and insurance (check Italy/Switzerland coverage)
  • European Health Insurance Cards
  • Travel insurance

Driving & Tolls:

  • French autoroutes: Pay tolls (card accepted)
  • Italy: Toll highways (card or cash)
  • Tunnel tolls: ~€50-70 one-way
  • Switzerland: Vignette CHF 40 required

Weather & Packing:

  • Expected temps: 10-18°C (50-64°F)
  • Pack layers - temperature varies throughout day
  • Waterproof jackets recommended
  • Comfortable walking shoes essential
