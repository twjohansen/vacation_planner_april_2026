Dijon & Lyon

Drive ~2 hours (Dijon to Lyon)
Overnight Lyon
Distance ~200 km

Morning Stop: Dijon

Location: 15 km from Château de Longecourt

Timing: 2-3 hours (9:00-11:30 AM)

Parking: Darcy parking (underground, central location)

Top 5 Things to See:

  • Ducal Palace (Palais des Ducs) - Place de la Libération, stunning semicircular square
    Climb Tour Philippe le Bon (316 steps) for panoramic views | FREE entry
    Apr-Oct 9:00-12:00, 13:45-17:30
  • Owl Trail (Parcours de la Chouette) - Follow brass owl markers through old town
    Rub the lucky owl sculpture on Notre-Dame church with left hand!
    Free map at tourist office or follow markers
  • Notre-Dame de Dijon - 13th century Gothic church with famous gargoyles
  • Rue des Forges - Medieval street with half-timbered houses
    Hôtel Chambellan at #34 (15th century mansion)
  • Les Halles Market - Designed by Gustave Eiffel (Tue-Sat mornings only)
    Try Dijon mustard from Moutarde Maille boutique nearby

Travel Notes

Compact old town - everything walkable in 30 minutes. Kids will enjoy following the owl trail. Grab coffee and croissant at Place de la Libération before exploring. Leave by 11:30-12:00 to reach Lyon by early afternoon.

Accommodation: Lyon

Hotel: Novotel Lyon Gerland Musée des Confluences ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Address: 70 Avenue Leclerc, 69007 Lyon

Tel: +33 4 72 71 11 11

Estimated cost: €110-140/night for family room

  • Easy access from highway (A7), practical for stopover
  • Family rooms with sofa bed (kids under 16 free in parents' room)
  • On-site restaurant (La Fabrik) with kids' menu
  • Game room and kids' corner
  • Parking: Free outdoor or €15/night covered
  • Tram T1 nearby for easy city center access (6 min to shopping center)
  • Near Musée des Confluences

Note

Pool is outdoor/seasonal - CLOSED in April. Good location for evening walk along Rhône River. 10-15 min drive to old town if dining out. Check-in typically from 14:00.

Exploring Lyon - Activities & Highlights

  • Vieux Lyon (Old Town) - UNESCO World Heritage site with Renaissance architecture
  • Traboules - Explore Lyon's famous hidden passageways
  • Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière - Hilltop basilica with panoramic city views
  • Parc de la Tête d'Or - Beautiful urban park with lake, zoo, and botanical gardens
  • Lyon Museum of Fine Arts
  • Evening stroll along the riverbanks - Lyon has beautiful illuminations

Dining

Lyon is considered the gastronomic capital of France. Consider a bouchon lyonnais (traditional restaurant).

Practical Tips

Lyon parking can be challenging - research parking garages near accommodation. Consider Lyon City Card for transport and museum access. Fourvière funicular makes hilltop access easy.

Destination Options (Turin / Parma / Piacenza)

Option 1: Turin

  • Mole Antonelliana - Iconic building housing National Cinema Museum

Route Considerations

Mont Blanc tunnel or Fréjus tunnel route options from Lyon. Check tunnel traffic and tolls in advance. Early April can still be cool in mountain areas - pack layers accordingly.

Northern Italy to Bologna

Drive ~1.5 hours
Overnight Bologna (3 nights)
Distance ~150 km

Exploring Bologna

  • Piazza Maggiore - Historic heart with Basilica di San Petronio
  • Due Torri (Two Towers) - Climb Torre degli Asinelli (498 steps) for views
  • Archiginnasio - Historic university building with anatomical theater
  • Porticoes of Bologna - UNESCO site, 38km of covered walkways
  • Santo Stefano complex - Medieval 'Seven Churches' complex
  • Quadrilatero market area - Vibrant food market district
  • Via dell'Indipendenza - Main shopping street

Dining in Bologna

  • Trattoria Anna Maria - Authentic traditional Bolognese cuisine
  • Osteria dell'Orsa - Popular with locals, excellent tagliatelle al ragù
  • All'Osteria Bottega - Wine bar with local dishes
  • Mercato di Mezzo - Food hall for casual dining
  • Gelato: Cremeria Funivia or Stefino

Must Try:

  • Tagliatelle al ragù (authentic Bolognese sauce)
  • Tortellini in brodo
  • Mortadella
  • Cotoletta alla bolognese

Note

Bologna has excellent train connections for day trips. Consider the Bologna Welcome card. Market visits are best in the morning.

Essential Planning Notes
